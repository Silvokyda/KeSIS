Keeping Track Of Student Performance

This feature focuses on continuously monitoring and recording students' academic progress over time. It provides a comprehensive view of a student's achievements, attendance, grades, and any other relevant information.

Easing Learner Assesment

Educational Fairness

The goal of this is to promote fairness and equity in education. It may include features like tracking and analyzing disparities in educational outcomes among different groups of students.

Sustainable suitable Learner Environment

This functionality is dedicated to creating and maintaining a conducive learning environment for students. It encompasses various aspects, such as safety, inclusivity, and access to resources.

About KeSIS

KeSIS is a web application designed to revolutionize the management and administration of student information in educational institutions across Kenya. It aims to provide a centralized platform for educators, administrators, parents, and students to access and utilize critical student data efficiently. KeSIS streamlines various educational processes, enhances learner assessment, and promotes educational fairness and sustainability.
